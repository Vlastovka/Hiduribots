About us
Our team was created on 31.10.2024. Originally there were two teams but due to lack of parts for both robots it was decided to create the team on 31.10.24'. The team was merged by combining the name Hiduri + Xbots. At ZSMS Jižní Elementary School.

Our team visited the town of Litoměřice in the early morning hours. We went in order to try what it is like at the competition and how it will look like.
